What is
a Burl?

NOUN / ETYMOLOGY UNKNOWN

A burl is a dense, rounded growth on a tree formed when the tree responds to stress—such as injury, infection, or environmental damage—by producing irregular grain patterns. Though abnormal, burls are prized for their unique beauty and strength in woodworking.
